## Getting Started

### How to Start Playing
**Category:** Getting Started  
**Tags:** first-time, tutorial, basics

## Gameplay Basics

### How to Feed Your Fish
**Category:** Gameplay Basics  
**Tags:** feeding, food, hunger

Feeding your fish is easy:

1. Tap the **floating action button (FAB)** in the bottom right corner
2. Select **"Feed"** from the menu
3. Food pellets will drop into the tank
4. Your fish will swim toward the food and eat it automatically
5. Watch the hunger stat increase!

**Tips:**
- Food takes about 6 seconds to fall to the bottom
- If food reaches the bottom uneaten, it will stay there until you clean the tank
- Feed your fish regularly to keep hunger high

---

### How to Clean the Tank
**Category:** Gameplay Basics  
**Tags:** cleaning, tank maintenance, cleanliness

Keeping your tank clean is important for your fish's health:

1. Tap the **floating action button (FAB)** in the bottom right
2. Select **"Clean"** from the menu
3. The tank will instantly become clean
4. Any uneaten food at the bottom will be removed
5. Watch the cleanliness stat increase!

**When to clean:**
- Clean when the tank background looks cloudy or dirty
- Clean when cleanliness stat drops below 70%
- Clean after feeding to remove uneaten food

## Troubleshooting

### My Fish Won't Eat the Food
**Category:** Troubleshooting  
**Tags:** feeding, food, collision, bug

If food drops but your fish doesn't eat it:

**Possible causes:**
1. **Food reached the bottom** - Food that falls to the bottom stays there until you clean
2. **Fish is moving away** - The fish might be swimming in a different area
3. **Collision detection** - The fish needs to be close to the food (within about 20% of tank size)

**Solutions:**
1. **Wait for the food to fall** - It takes about 6 seconds
2. **Feed again** - Drop more food; the fish will swim toward it
3. **Clean the tank** - This removes uneaten food at the bottom
4. **Check fish position** - The fish moves automatically toward food

**If the problem persists:**
- Restart the app
- Make sure you're tapping the Feed button correctly
- Check that the hunger stat is updating

---

### My Stats Aren't Updating
**Category:** Troubleshooting  
**Tags:** stats, bug, not updating

If your fish's stats don't seem to be updating:

**Check these things:**
1. **Wait a moment** - Stats update after actions complete
2. **Check the stat bars** - Look at the visual bars, not just numbers
3. **Feed/clean again** - Make sure the action completed successfully

**Common issues:**
- **Food didn't reach fish** - Clean the tank and try feeding again
- **Action didn't trigger** - Make sure you tapped the button correctly
- **App needs refresh** - Close and reopen the app

**If stats still don't update:**
- Force close the app and reopen
- Check that you have the latest version
- Contact support if the problem continues

### I Lost My Progress / Data Reset
**Category:** Troubleshooting  
**Tags:** data loss, progress, reset, backup

If your fish's progress seems to have reset:

**Important:** Pixel Fish Tank stores all data locally on your device.

**Possible causes:**
1. **App was uninstalled** - Uninstalling removes all local data
2. **App data was cleared** - Clearing app data in Android settings removes progress
3. **Device storage issue** - Low storage can cause data loss
4. **App update issue** - Rare, but updates can sometimes cause issues

**Prevention:**
- **Don't uninstall** the app if you want to keep progress
- **Don't clear app data** in Android settings
- **Keep device storage free** - Ensure you have enough space
- **Regular backups** - Future versions may support backup/restore

**Recovery:**
Unfortunately, if data is lost, it cannot be recovered in v1. Future versions may include backup features.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
**Category:** Technical Support  
**Tags:** FAQ, common questions

**Q: Do I need internet to play?**  
A: No! Pixel Fish Tank works completely offline. All data is stored on your device.

**Q: Can I have multiple fish?**  
A: Not in v1, but multiple tanks may be added in future updates.

**Q: Will my progress transfer to a new device?**  
A: Not in v1. Future versions may include backup/restore features.

**Q: Is the app free?**  
A: Check the app store listing for current pricing information.

**Q: Can I reset my fish and start over?**  
A: Currently, you would need to clear app data (which deletes everything). A reset feature may be added in the future.

**Q: Why does my fish's mood change?**  
A: Mood is based on hunger and cleanliness stats. As stats change, the mood updates automatically.

**Q: How often should I check on my fish?**  
A: At least once per day is recommended, but you can check in as often as you like!

**Q: What happens if I don't play for a while?**  
A: Stats will decay over time, but your fish won't die. Just feed and clean when you return!
