Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pixel Fish Tank?

+

Pixel Fish Tank is a cozy Android virtual pet game inspired by classic Tamagotchi games. You care for a cute pixel-art fish by feeding it, cleaning its tank, playing mini-games, and decorating its environment as your fish grows and levels up.

What age group is this game for?

+

Pixel Fish Tank is designed for ages 8 and up. It's perfect for casual players, daily check-in players, and anyone who enjoys relaxing virtual pet games.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

+

No! Pixel Fish Tank is fully playable offline. All your progress, including fish stats, coins, decorations, and daily streaks, is saved locally on your device. You don't need an internet connection to enjoy the game.

How do I take care of my fish?

+

You need to manage three key stats: hunger, cleanliness, and happiness. Feed your fish regularly, clean the tank when it gets dirty, and decorate the tank to keep your fish happy. Your fish's mood will reflect the quality of care you provide!

What are mini-games?

+

Mini-games are fun, repeatable games you can play to earn coins and XP for your fish. Challenge yourself to beat your high scores and earn more rewards!

How does the decoration system work?

+

You can customize your tank with various decorations like plants, rocks, and toys. Decorations can be purchased with coins earned from mini-games and daily tasks. They make your tank unique and can also affect your fish's happiness!

What are daily tasks and streaks?

+

Daily tasks are special objectives you can complete each day to earn rewards. Maintaining a daily streak by playing regularly helps you earn bonus rewards and keeps your fish happy!

Can I get notifications to remind me to care for my fish?

+

Yes! You can enable optional notifications for feeding reminders and daily check-ins. These can be customized in the app settings to help you maintain your care routine.

Is my progress saved?

+

Yes! All your progress is automatically saved locally on your device. This includes your fish's stats (hunger, cleanliness, happiness, level, XP), coins, inventory, tank decorations, daily streaks, and mini-game high scores.

Do I need to create an account?

+

No account is required! Pixel Fish Tank works completely offline and doesn't require any user accounts. All data is stored locally on your device. Future versions may include optional backup features via Google Drive.

What platforms is the game available on?

+

Currently, Pixel Fish Tank is available for Android devices (Android 7.0 and higher). An iOS version may be available in the future.

How do I level up my fish?

+

Your fish gains XP by completing tasks, playing mini-games, and receiving proper care. As your fish levels up, it will visually grow and become more expressive. Keep taking good care of your fish to help it reach higher levels!

How can I contact support or report an issue?

+

  • Email us at tickets@pixel-fish-tank.p.tawk.email
  • Use the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of this page
